# Feature: Budget Management

## What
Enables users to manage and review budget items for each project, including total budget calculation and category breakdowns.

## Why
To ensure projects comply with funding requirements and help users plan and justify their budgets effectively.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Users can add, edit, and remove budget items
- [ ] Users can see total and category-wise budget
- [ ] Budget is linked to project sections and requirements
